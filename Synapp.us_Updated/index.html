Single Sign-On (SSO) & Identity Management

The SSO/Identity Management feature streamlines how you and your team access SynApp and your Telegram resources. Here's how it helps:

  • One Login for Everything: Use a single account to securely access all SynApp features and tools. No need to remember multiple passwords!
  • Seamless Access: Log in once and stay connected to all your Telegram groups and channels without interruptions.
  • Manage User Roles: Assign roles and permissions to team members, ensuring everyone has the right access.
  • Secure and Reliable: Protect your data with industry-standard authentication, keeping your information safe.
  • Integration Ready: Works with popular identity systems, so it fits smoothly into your existing setup.

With SSO and Identity Management, accessing and managing your Telegram resources is simpler, faster, and more secure.

CRM Integration

The CRM Integration feature connects SynApp with your corporate Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system (like HubSpot). This ensures that all interactions managed by the bot are captured and organized in your CRM. Key benefits include:

  • Track Customer Interactions: Automatically log all messages, inquiries, and actions processed by the bot into your CRM, ensuring a complete history of customer engagement.
  • Seamless Integration: Works with popular CRM platforms like HubSpot, Salesforce, and others, enabling effortless syncing of data.
  • Centralized Insights: Gain a unified view of all customer interactions within your CRM, no matter where they originate.
  • Enhanced Customer Management: Keep track of customer touchpoints to deliver personalized and efficient service.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Automate data entry, reduce manual effort, and ensure no interactions are missed or lost.

With CRM Integration, SynApp empowers your team to better manage and understand customer relationships, boosting productivity and service quality.
